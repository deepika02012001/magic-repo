Dubrovnik, Croatia

This charming city lies on the shores of the Adriatic Sea. Nicknamed the “Pearl of the Adriatic,” Dubrovnik has been a treasured seaport and cultural center since the Middle Ages. As such, it’s one of the most beautiful European cities that you should visit in 2021.

Strasbourg, France

Strasbourg is the capital of the Alsace region in eastern France. Cherished for its vibrant timber houses, Strasbourg looks like a scene straight out of a fairytale. It’s no wonder why it’s considered one of the most beautiful European cities.

Florence, Italy

Florence (Firenze) is the capital of Italy’s Tuscany region. It’s the notable birthplace of the Renaissance, which is strikingly visible throughout the city. Florence is also known for its world-class art, fashion, and cuisine.

Santorini, Greece

Santorini is a Greek island in the Aegean Sea comprised of several tiny villages. Its most popular settlements include Oia, Fira, and Imerovigli. For the purposes of this article, I’ll consider Santorini a city, as these settlements form one municipality.

Lisbon, Portugal

As Portugal’s coastal capital, Lisbon is best known for its colorful buildings, cobbled streets, and seven imposing hills. With no shortage of steep streets and cable cars, Lisbon is the old world doppelgänger of San Francisco.

Venice, Italy

Venice is the capital of Italy’s Veneto region, and comprises more than 100 small islands in the Adriatic Sea. Nicknamed La Serenissima (the most serene), Venice is one of the most beautiful cities and UNESCO Sites in Europe.

Salzburg, Austria

Salzburg is a picturesque city in western Austria that borders Germany and the Eastern Alps. It’s one of the prettiest cities in Europe on many accounts.As the birthplace of Mozart, Salzburg is often branded as the ‘capital of music’.

Paris, France

As the capital of France, Paris is one of the most visited cities in the world. Paris is a global leader in the arts, fashion, and cuisine.The Parisian cityscape is defined by world-class attractions such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum.

Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg, long considered one of the prettiest cities in Europe, is a magical medieval masterpiece in northern Bavaria. Dating back to the Holy Roman Empire, Nuremberg was an attractive residence for kings and artists alike.

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is such an incredible European city that you should add to your bucket list.It’s the capital and cultural center of Spain’s Catalonia region. Attracting 32 million tourists last year, Barcelona is the most visited city in one of the most visited European countries.
